Web pages can often look like some mysterious complex entities. But the more you dig into web development the more you realize that the basic of a web page are super simple

Then you dig deeper and you realize that everything is super complicate so you decide to stick to your superficial knowledge which is enough to impress everyone around you.

What happens when I enter a website's address

When you enter the name of your website in your Browser(like Firefox, Chrome, Internet Explorer...), you're asking The Internet to contact another computer.

That other computer (aka server) then sends you some files. Think about when you're navigating in your folder to find a specific file. For example, you might store your last vacation pictures under Pictures/Holiday/Summer2020/awesome-selfie.png

.

The way you would navigate to this image on your computer is similar to how a lot of websites work. hardcorejs.fun works that way. You enter:hardcorejs.fun/javascript and it GETs you the file at that location. This type of website is called a Static Website because no matter who you are we will always send you the same file.
